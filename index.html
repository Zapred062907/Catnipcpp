WHY DO CATS LOVE CATNIP?

Cats love catnip because it contains a chemical compound called nepatalactone, which stimulates receptors in their brains, resulting in a euphoric and playful reaction.

IS CATNIP BAD FOR YOUR CAT?

Catnip is generally safe for most cats and can be a fun and enjoyable experience for them. It's a plant from the mint family that contains a compound called nepetalactone, which can trigger a temporary behavioral reaction in cats.
